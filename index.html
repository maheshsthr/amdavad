The Rich Historical Legacy of Ahmedabad

Ahmedabad, often called Amdavad, is a city with a rich historical and cultural heritage. It was Founded in 1411 AD by Sultan Ahmed Shah of Gujarat Sultanate, after whom the city is named. Located on the Banks of Sabarmati River, Ahmedabad has evolved into a blend of historical charm and modern development.
